About us

"We" (me) a one man team that want to show you another facet of the world around us. In everything we create or build we feel the presence of what we saw in nature, a big or small world with a lot of secrets (some not yet discovered) that we pass in a hurry. We are in a hurry, but when we create something, the inspiration makes us think of: "like, if it were ... like what we saw in the park some timeS ago". We offer you the opportunity to see the world through the lens of our camera: moreover, you can visit our blog and youtube channel for a lot of information and news about this world.

Close up and macro photography will give you the opportunity to have a look at our photo gallery, to make an impression and if you are interested, you can subscribe to our news by clicking on the "SUBSCRIBE" button on the page.
